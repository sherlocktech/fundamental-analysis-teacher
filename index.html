Fundamental Analysis Teacher

Welcome to the Fundamental Analysis Teacher, or FAT. This website will teach you how to value financial assets such as US Teasury Bonds or US stocks.

Let's start with the bonds.

Bonds mature in a certain amount of time at which point the face value is paid to the holder of the bond. Coupon payments, if there are any, are also paid periodically to the holder.

Bonds are generally purchased for less than face value. The money gained from a bond sale today is more valuable to the seller than what the seller has agreed to pay at maturity.

The same time value of money principle is true for the buyer of the bond and that is exactly why we need to determine the required rate of return.
